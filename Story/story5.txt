With the runes deciphered they left at dawn, heading to Erebor. For many miles they travelled and saw no man. Until they saw monstrous figures in combat. ‘Those are giants, stone-giants!’ – one dwarf yelled. In fear they watched, mountain-high giants, turn rock into dust. The company of dwarfs had to decide what to do, to stay alive. Should they take refuge into the cave, or continue the trail to Erebor?
